Welcome to Chef YumYum!

Chef YumYum is a new meal service that offers more than just recipes. Of course, you can start by choosing our recipe-only service, and tell us your favorite ingredients and foods so that we can suggest a meal plan for you that will include the healthiest and most delicious things for you and your fmaily, but we have many other options as well! Choose from our prepared meal ingredients kits or from our fully-prepared and cooked meals delivered to your door by local drivers.

Our company began as a small, chef-run concept with the belief that we could help our friends feed their families the food that they loved, in the time that they had, without needing to be professionally trained as chefs. We worked together performing research for three years, first with our friends and family, them with other locals, then across the country, to make sure that we understood the needs of our audeince. We want you to love what you eat!

Chef YumYum is small but mighty, and we are here to help! You can find free recipes here on our site to get you started, and to give you an idea of what we offer our customers. We want you to feel like family, and our family eats very well. YumYum!
